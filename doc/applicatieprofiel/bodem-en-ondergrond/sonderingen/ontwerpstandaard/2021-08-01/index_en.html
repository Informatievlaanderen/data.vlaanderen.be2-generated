This document describes an application profile, in this case CPTs (Application Profile). The application profile specifies a semantic data model covering a set of usecases. The specification consists of terms with their constraints (cardinalities, codelists, usage guidelines).

Summary

The application profile Groundwater Monitoring Network explains how the terminology in this domain should be used to exchange data about Observations carried out by means of CPT.

The application profile builds on the OSLO Sensors and Sampling application profile, among other things.

By CPT we mean the vertical sampling of Soil and Subsoil by inserting a Probe into the soil.

This probe measures the variation with the depth of certain characteristics of soil and subsoil, such as stratification, load-bearing capacity, pollution, etc.

The specialisations in this application profile mainly focus on a particular type of CPT: variants of the Cone Penetration Test (CPT) or driving a cone into the ground in which certain characteristics of the soil and subsoil can be indirectly or directly identified along the way
