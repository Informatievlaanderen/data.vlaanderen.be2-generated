This document describes a vocabulary, in this case Soil drilling (Vocabulary). A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, namely classes and properties, whose meaning is recorded through a label and definition. For terms defined in this domain, additional formal agreements are available. For external terms, the formal agreements are copied from the external source.

Summary

The Soil Drilling vocabulary includes terminology related to drilling or sampling the soil.

To this end, it specialises in terms of the vocabulary Sensors and Sampling.

These include Soil Drilling, Borehole and Soil Sample as a special case of Sampling, Spatial Sampling Object and Sample respectively.

Drill merthode and drill are also defined and characteristics of the Soil Drilling (e.g. driller and driller or Legal Framework) or properties of the Borehole such as formwork and diameter.

4. Classes

Class Borehole diameter

Type Class
URI https://data.vlaanderen.be/ns/grondboringen#Boorgatdiameter

  • http://def.isotc211.org/iso19123/2005/DiscreteCoverages#CV_DiscreteCurveCoverage
Definition Diameter of the borehole as a function of the depth.

Type Class
