This document describes a vocabulary, in this case Vocabulary Mobility Budget. A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, namely classes and properties, whose meaning is recorded through a label and definition. For terms defined in this domain, additional formal agreements are available. For external terms, the formal agreements are copied from the external source.

Summary

The objective of this Vocabulary is to standardise the flow of information about the mobility budget (for citizens). This application profile was developed as part of the CoT project of the cities of Hasselt, Leuven and Antwerp to arrive at a Mobility Budget for citizens.

The vocabulary reuses terms and the way they are applied from the following standards, among others:

  • OSLO Generic
  • OSLO Person
  • OSLO Mobility: trips and offers
  • OSLO Organization

To reduce the traffic burden in the busy city centres, the mobility budget has been created. This budget for citizens encourages them to take more sustainable alternatives than the traditional car. This budget can be allocated by a local government, another government body, local merchants and mobility services themselves. All of them can send a budget to certain citizens in the form of cash discounts, free rides or other promotions. Users can then purchase a ticket for themselves or other people to get around. There are also conditions for this budget, budgets can be limited per age group, time aspect, location,...
