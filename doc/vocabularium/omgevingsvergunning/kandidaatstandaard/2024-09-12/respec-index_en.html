Legal Work

URI https://data.vlaanderen.be/ns/omgevingsvergunning#JuridischWerk
Definition Works formatted for legal purposes.
Label Legal Work
Usage note Includes, among other things, legislation and regulations, public and private legal acts of courts, administrative bodies and other legal entities. This also includes legal doctrine, as well as legal typologies (Legal Category). Technocratic instruments, such as standards, specifications and implementations thereof that fulfil a legal function are covered by this entity. A Legal Work is usually reflected in one or more Documents. The legal value of a Legal Work depends, among other things, on the existence of an authentic version (Document) that has been published in accordance with the provisions laid down. Broader than http://data.europa.eu/eli/ontology#LegalResource which is limited to legislative works.

Legal subject

URI https://data.vlaanderen.be/ns/omgevingsvergunning#Rechtssubject
Definition Entity that is subject to the law, that is or may be the bearer of rights and obligations (Legal Relationships), and that is considered capable of carrying out legal acts or having them carried out in its name (on its behalf).
Label Legal subject
Usage note Includes both natural persons (people) and legal entities. Includes both private and public entities. Can enter into contracts, own property, be held liable for crimes, and so on. In the context of environmental permits: the applicant for a permit, the licensing authority, the Council for Permit Disputes, the operator of a nuisance establishment, the executor of urban development activities, the reporter of an irregularity, and so on. Legal subject is similar in concept to OSLO generic:Agent (also collective term for natural and legal persons and defined as "someone who can act or have an effect"), but is narrower than Agent and specific to the legal context (including administrative law, including permit policy), and is therefore preferred in the context of environmental permits because of this legal connotation.
Parent http://purl.org/dc/terms/Agent
