This document describes a vocabulary, in this case Sensors and Sampling (Vocabulary). A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, namely classes and properties, whose meaning is recorded through a label and definition. For terms defined in this domain, additional formal agreements are available. For external terms, the formal agreements are copied from the external source.

Summary

The Sensors and Sampling vocabulary contains the terminology needed to describe the observation instruments used to perform Observations and also to document the activity of taking representative spatial or physical samples.

Most of it is external terminology based on the W3C SSN Ontology and extensions. These build on terminology from ISO 19156:2011 Observations & Measurements. Furthermore, a class was reused from the SAREF Ontology to describe Aircraft.

2. Conformity

An exchange of data, by whatever means, is subject to this vocabulary when it uses the terminology (classes and properties) in a manner consistent with the semantics established in the latest version of the specification (domain, scope, definition and usage) and does not use terminology from other vocabularies as an alternative to the proposed terminology included in this vocabulary.
