Dependencies

This document describes a vocabulary, in this case Vocabulary Culture and Youth Infrastructure. A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, namely classes and properties, whose meaning is recorded through a label and definition. For terms defined in this domain, additional formal agreements are available. For external terms, the formal agreements are copied from the external source.

Summary

This vocabulary defines a number of terms to describe Culture & Youth Infrastructure.

The application profile reuses terms and the way they are applied from the application profile OSLO-Organization, OSLO-CulturalHeritage:Object, OSLO Person, OSLO-Consent, OSLO Grant and OSLO Association, Actual OSLO-Purpose-Driven Digital Transformation, OSLO Service, OSLO observations and measurements, OSLO Building and OSLO Plot.

2. Conformity

An exchange of data, by whatever means, is subject to this vocabulary when it uses the terminology (classes and properties) in a manner consistent with the semantics established in the latest version of the specification (domain, scope, definition and usage) and does not use terminology from other vocabularies as an alternative to the proposed terminology included in this vocabulary.
