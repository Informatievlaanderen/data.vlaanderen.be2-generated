This document describes a vocabulary, in this case OSLO Utilities Meters (Vocabulary). A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, namely classes and properties, whose meaning is recorded through a label and definition. For terms defined in this domain, additional formal agreements are available. For external terms, the formal agreements are copied from the external source.

Summary

The "OSLO Digital Water Meter" vocabulary defines terms specifically for the management and monitoring of water consumption via digital water meters. It describes characteristic features of digital water meters, including the components such as consumption observation, sensors and meters. These terms have been adapted to accurately describe the (drinking) water consumption. This vocabulary is tailored to OSLO models IMKL (Information Model Cables and Pipes), focused on the specific requirements and functionalities of digital water meters.
